Are you an Introvert or an Extrovert?

This quiz tests for the cognitive (Jungian) definitions and NOT the common definitions (sociability).

Jungian definitions are concerned about the individual's perception of life whereas the common definition is more concerned about behaviors and social interactions of the individual. For example: You may be an introvert according to the Jungian definition and an ambivert according to the common definition.
